Based in Bangalore, India

I make music perfecting in sound design and production in direction towards soundtracks for media, movies, video games, and television, with a strong focus on film scoring. My musical style is exploratory and experimental, blending intricate sound design with storytelling elements to craft emotionally resonant and visually inspired compositions.

My journey began with an early passion for the flute, which later expanded to the keyboard and acoustic guitar. Transitioning into music production, I found influences and developed a distinct approach centered on synthesizers, layered harmonics, and unique soundscapes that merge creativity with technical precision.

Shaped by audience feedback, my work now prioritizes instrumental music tailored to enhance narrative experiences. By aligning melodies with imagery, I aim to create immersive soundtracks that resonate deeply with listeners and complement visual storytelling.
