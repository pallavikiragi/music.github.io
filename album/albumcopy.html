Places | EP


Release Date: December 31, 2020
Distributor: Horus Music

The project explores a sci-fi and electronic score-inspired sound, featuring a dynamic blend of ambient, melodic, and hard-hitting tracks. Entirely instrumental, the compositions are built using soft synth layers, gritty basslines, and textured drum patterns. The EP showcases a cinematic sonic journey—most notably in tracks like “Blade Runner”, which draws inspiration from the iconic film’s futuristic imagery and sound design, and “Conflict”, which captures emotional tension through immersive sound layering and automation.
This release reflects my early experimentation with atmospheric storytelling through electronic music production and continues to be a defining point in my sound direction.

Insights
| Album


Release Date: January 5, 2024
Distributor: Horus Music

Crafted over two years, this 12-track instrumental album reflects a personal journey of creative growth and emotional exploration. Born during the pandemic from a period of mental block, it rekindles inspiration through gratitude and cinematic influences. Blending experimental electronic music with film scoring techniques, the album is structured in three phases—starting with ambient orchestral sounds, transitioning to synth-heavy compositions, and concluding with rhythmic, melodic tracks. Designed to suit a range of moods and moments, each piece evokes vivid imagery and emotional depth.

The album begins with a transitional piece marking a shift in production style, followed by an ambient track inspired by Middle Eastern and Indian cultures. It moves into a nature-driven, adventurous composition evoking fantasy and exploration, then a reflective piece about memories shaping the future. Two interludes serve as transitions—first from organic to electronic sounds, and later to more melodic, drum-heavy elements. The central tracks explore digital and urban landscapes with synth-heavy, cinematic soundscapes, gritty rock-infused energy, and street-culture influences. The final tracks embrace EDM-inspired rhythms and conclude with a glitchy, experimental piece symbolizing a new chapter.

Highlight tracks: Purple Phantom, Sands Unfold, CityOS
